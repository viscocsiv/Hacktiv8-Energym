Work hard, play hard, live well

Also applied in the context of sports and physical fitness. "Work hard" reflects the commitment to regular training, engaging in rigorous workouts, and planning fitness programs with a focus on achieving excellent results in sports. "Play hard" portrays the importance of participating in games or enjoyable physical activities to maintain enthusiasm and motivation in sports. Lastly, "Live well" represents the health and well-being benefits derived from an active and healthy lifestyle, as well as the sense of well-being that sports provided. Thus, through this approach, one can achieve a good balance between dedication to sports, enjoyment, and overall health in your life.
